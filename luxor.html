Luxor Attractions

Karnak Temple

One of the largest religious complexes in the world, dedicated to the Theban Triad.

Luxor Temple

A grand temple located on the east bank of the Nile, dedicated to the Theban Triad.

Valley of the Kings

The burial place of many of Egypt's Pharaohs, including Tutankhamun.

Hatshepsut Temple

A stunning mortuary temple built for the female Pharaoh Hatshepsut.

Luxor Museum

A museum showcasing antiquities from Luxor and the surrounding area.
