Aswan Attractions

Philae Temple

Beautiful temple complex dedicated to the goddess Isis, located on an island in the Nile.

High Dam

One of the world’s largest embankment dams, vital for Egypt’s irrigation and electricity.

Unfinished Obelisk

A huge ancient obelisk left partially carved in the bedrock, showcasing ancient engineering.

Nubian Museum

Modern museum highlighting the culture and history of Nubia and southern Egypt.
